# Behind the Reels Website PRD

## Product Summary

Behind the Reels is a mental health storytelling and community platform for young Africans. The website should extend the existing social media presence into a deeper, more structured home for stories, dialogues, outreach work, and practical mental health resources.

The central idea is: behind every reel is a real story. The platform should help young Africans move from scrolling and relating to actually reflecting, learning, sharing, and finding support.

## Goals

- Present Behind the Reels as a credible, youth-led mental health community for Africans.
- Make storytelling the emotional center of the website.
- Help non-technical admins publish and manage stories, resources, events, outreach updates, and homepage content through a CMS.
- Convert Instagram/social visitors into community members, newsletter subscribers, volunteers, story contributors, partners, or donors.
- Make the brand feel warm, trustworthy, culturally aware, and modern without feeling clinical.

## Target Users

- Young Africans navigating anxiety, pressure, loneliness, identity, family expectations, school, work, faith, relationships, and healing.
- People who discover the community through Instagram reels and want deeper content.
- Volunteers, partners, schools, NGOs, and mental health advocates.
- Non-technical content admins who need to manage the website without developer support.

## Core Message

Behind the Reels creates honest mental health conversations for young Africans through storytelling, dialogues, and community outreach.

Primary headline direction:

Behind Every Reel Is a Real Story

Supporting copy direction:

A fast-growing mental health community helping young Africans speak honestly about pressure, identity, family, anxiety, healing, and hope.

## Website Structure

### Home

The homepage should immediately communicate the mission, then guide visitors into stories, resources, community, and impact.

Key sections:

- Header navigation
- Hero with mission, CTA buttons, and human visual treatment
- Impact strip: 1M+ reached, community growth, stories shared, outreach moments
- Featured Stories
- Dialogues and Conversations
- Outreach and Impact
- Resource Hub
- Community CTA
- Footer

### Stories

Editorial hub for personal essays, reflections, interviews, and educational articles.

CMS fields:

- Title
- Slug
- Excerpt
- Cover image
- Author
- Category
- Body content
- Published date
- Featured flag
- Related Instagram URL
- SEO title and description

Suggested categories:

- Family and Silence
- Anxiety and Pressure
- Identity and Belonging
- Faith and Mental Health
- Men and Mental Health
- School, Work, and Burnout
- Love and Relationships
- Healing and Hope

### Dialogues

A home for live conversations, community questions, interviews, panels, and social media discussion recaps.

CMS fields:

- Title
- Date
- Speaker or guest
- Summary
- Recording link
- Cover image
- Key takeaways
- Related stories/resources

### Outreaches

Show offline community work and impact.

CMS fields:

- Title
- Location
- Date
- Summary
- Gallery
- Impact stats
- Partner organizations
- Testimonial quote

### Resources

Practical mental health guides written in a culturally sensitive voice.

Example resource topics:

- How to support a friend who is struggling
- What to do when you feel emotionally numb
- Talking to African parents about mental health
- Understanding anxiety beyond overthinking
- Finding help without shame

### About

Explain the origin, mission, approach, and team behind Behind the Reels.

### Submit Your Story

A guided form for community members to share experiences safely.

### Partner / Donate

A page for schools, NGOs, brands, and individuals who want to support outreach, events, campaigns, or community care work.

## Visual Direction

The design should feel like a safe room, an editorial journal, and a youth movement.

Recommended style:

- Warm, human, editorial, grounded, modern
- Calm but not dull
- Youthful but not childish
- African without relying on cliches
- Story-first, with real human imagery where possible

Color direction:

- Deep charcoal for text and grounding
- Warm off-white background
- Leaf green as the primary brand color
- Sunrise yellow or coral as a highlight
- Soft clay or muted rose for emotional warmth

Typography direction:

- Expressive serif or high-character display font for hero/editorial moments
- Clean sans-serif for body text and interface
- Strong hierarchy for scan-friendly content
